Introduction to Primate Data Exploration and Linear Modeling with R

Contributors: Raisa Hernandez-Pacheco, Alexandra L. Bland, Alexis A. Diaz, Alexandra G Rosati, Stephanie J. Gonzalez

Description: Training modules for data exploration, visualization and modeling with R programming language using authentic demographic and cogntive data of rhesus macaques at the Cayo Santiago Biological Field Station. For more information about these training modules and the data needed to implement them, visit our publication in QUBES (https://qubeshub.org/publications/4418/1)
